Welcome to DM Lab

Dr. Heidar (Kourosh) Davoudi, Associate Professor at Ontario Tech University, leads innovative research in Machine Learning, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, and AI. With a PhD from York University and postdoctoral work at the University of Waterloo, he drives AI advancements through interdisciplinary research and collaborations, addressing complex challenges across industries.

Explore Our Research

Our Research Areas

At Prof. Davoudi's lab, we explore a variety of advanced research topics that have wide-ranging implications across industries and academic fields. Our focus is on developing cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems through rigorous scientific inquiry and collaboration. Below are some of the key areas we are actively researching:

Deep Learning

We are pushing the boundaries of deep learning by developing innovative models and architectures for image processing, natural language understanding, and autonomous systems. Our projects focus on areas such as convolutional neural networks, generative adversarial networks (GANs), and transformers.

Reinforcement Learning

Our research on reinforcement learning aims to create algorithms that enable systems to learn optimal behaviors through trial and error. We are working on multi-agent systems, decision-making under uncertainty, and applications in robotics and game theory.

Graph Mining

Graph mining plays a critical role in understanding complex, interconnected systems. Our research focuses on detecting patterns, identifying influential nodes, and applying graph mining techniques to domains like social networks, biological networks, and cybersecurity.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

We are advancing natural language processing by developing models for language generation, understanding, and translation. Our focus includes sentiment analysis, question-answering systems, and the ethical implications of AI in language processing.
