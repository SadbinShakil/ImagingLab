Application Instructions

If you're interested in joining Prof. Kourosh Davoudi's Lab as a graduate student (PhD, MSc), please follow these instructions carefully:

How to Apply

  • Step 1: Prepare an updated CV detailing your academic background, research experience, and relevant skills.
  • Step 2: Write a cover letter explaining why you're interested in Prof. Davoudi's lab and how your skills align with the research areas of the lab. Be sure to mention any relevant publications or research projects you have worked on.
  • Step 3: Submit your CV and cover letter via email to Prof. Davoudi's Email.

Additional Requirements

  • Ensure that your CV is in PDF format.
  • Use a professional subject line for your email, such as "Graduate Application - [Your Name]".
  • If applicable, include links to your published papers, GitHub repositories, or research portfolio.
  • If you're applying from outside Canada, mention your visa or residency status.

Important Deadlines

There are no strict deadlines for applications; however, it is recommended to apply at least three months before the start of the desired semester. Admissions typically occur for the Fall, Winter, and Summer semesters.

Interview Process

If your application is shortlisted, you will be invited for an interview. The interview will focus on your research background, your understanding of machine learning, and how your goals align with the lab's research.
